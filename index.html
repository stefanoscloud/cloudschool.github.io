Cloud, AI, Cybersecurity — Made Understandable

CloudSchool is an educational community offering free and premium training in cloud computing, AI/ML, cybersecurity, DevOps, and modern infrastructure — built for Greece and the world.

Our Mission

We combine formal technical education (the how) with technical literacy (the why). We help close the digital skills gap in Greece by offering structured learning paths, hands-on labs, community documentation, and valid career guidance — always keeping the human at the center of the AI era.
